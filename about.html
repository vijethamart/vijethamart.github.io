Welcome to Vijetha Mart

Vijetha Mart (doing business as Desi Chowrastha) is a one-stop destination offering authentic Indian groceries, premium halal meat, and a restaurant serving fresh Hyderabadi cuisine. Our mission is to bring the authentic flavors of India to your doorstep with quality products and exceptional service.

Our Story

With market research and proven industry experience, we launched "Desi Chowrastha" with a vision to bring the authentic "Chowrastha" culture to every city in the USA. In India, "Chowrasthas" (the intersection of four streets) were central gathering places that defined culture and brought people together.

The word "Desi" refers to the people, cultures, and products of the Indian subcontinent and their diaspora, tracing its origin specifically to the people from the countries of India, Pakistan, and Bangladesh.

Our logo is inspired by the intersection of four streets. The orange color circle fuels appetite and amplifies senses, showing connection to our food that invokes the taste buds.

Who We Serve

The USA has the largest number of immigrants from all over the world, with the Indian migrant population exploring and expanding in the last 5-6 years. We are creating a one-stop shop for groceries, halal meat, and restaurant concept that caters not only to the largest Indian population in the US but to everyone open to exploring food with complex flavors and spices.

Through Food, Fresh Groceries, Halal Meat, and a Vibrant Environment, we aim to provide our customers with Fun, Full & Feel for Life. We're bringing back the culture of "Chowrastha" and Indianness to the South Asian community with groceries, halal meat, and mouth-watering food that tantalize the taste buds.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

Bring back the culture of "Chowrastha" and Indianness to the South Asian community with groceries, halal meat and mouth-watering food that tantalize the taste buds.

We envision a "Chowrastha" in every city of USA for people to come together and celebrate the taste of Indianness.

Our Mission

To provide a One stop shop that caters to the needs of the South Asian community.

To provide a refined environment and experience for people to come together and celebrate the flavors of food.

To deliver scrumptious food that smacks your lips, catering to South Asian, local and international foodies.

What We Offer

Restaurant

Authentic Hyderabadi cuisine featuring signature dishes like Dum Biryani, Irani Chai, and traditional Indian delicacies prepared with authentic spices and recipes.

Indian Grocery

Fresh produce, pantry essentials like Indian spices, rice, flours, snacks, beverages, and more to bring authentic Indian flavors to your kitchen.

Halal Meat Shop

Premium quality halal chicken, lamb/mutton, beef, and goat meat cuts prepared according to halal standards for your cooking needs.

Franchise
